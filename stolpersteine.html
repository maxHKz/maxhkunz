Stolpersteine

berlin

For this years International Holocaust Remembrance day, I visualised an art project by artist Gunter Demnig called Stolpersteine, which is located in Berlin. Stolpersteine are concrete blocks with a brass top, replacing a pavement stone in front of the last residence of a victim of the Nazis. The brass top is engraved with their name and their fate.

The data includes the victims' names, the address, birth year and the neighbourhood the stone is located in.
